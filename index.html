About Us & Our History

The Ananda College Mathematics Society has a storied history that dates back to its founding in 1953 within the esteemed Ananda College in Colombo, Sri Lanka. The society's inception was driven by the collective enthusiasm of a group of dedicated students and teachers who shared a profound love for mathematics and a vision to nurture mathematical talent. In its early years, the society primarily focused on organizing math-related events, competitions, and lectures within the school. These activities quickly gained popularity and contributed to a deeper appreciation for mathematics among the student body. The society encouraged students to engage in mathematical research, fostering a culture of curiosity and exploration. Throughout the years, the Ananda College Mathematics Society evolved and expanded its reach. It began participating in national and international math competitions, achieving remarkable success and bringing recognition to both the society and the school. Its members consistently garnered top honors, highlighting the society's commitment to academic excellence. The society also played a crucial role in promoting mathematical education beyond Ananda College. It organized outreach programs, tutoring sessions, and workshops for students from various schools, further cementing its reputation as a beacon of mathematical knowledge and learning in Sri Lanka. Today, the Ananda College Mathematics Society continues to thrive, continuing its tradition of excellence in mathematics education and research. It remains a vital part of the school's legacy, inspiring students to explore the fascinating world of mathematics and fostering a spirit of intellectual curiosity and achievement.


Our Mission: To cultivate mathematical excellence through collaborative learning, innovative problem-solving, and academic enrichment.


Our Vision: To create future mathematicians, scientists, and thinkers who will shape tomorrow's innovations.

Brief History

1953

The Ananda College Mathematics Society was founded at Ananda College, Colombo, by a group of passionate students and teachers with a shared vision to nurture mathematical talent.

Early Years

The society focused on organizing mathematics competitions, lectures, and school-level activities, fostering curiosity and appreciation for mathematics among students.

Growth Phase

The society expanded its reach by participating in national and international mathematics competitions, achieving notable success and recognition.

Community Impact

Outreach programs, workshops, and tutoring sessions were introduced to support students from other schools and promote mathematics education across Sri Lanka.

Present Day

The Ananda College Mathematics Society continues to thrive as a cornerstone of academic excellence, inspiring generations of students to explore mathematics.
